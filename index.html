Research Experience

2026

Stage-wise NWP Integration for Wind Power Forecasting

Principal Investigator · Undergraduate Innovation Training Program (大创)

Investigated how Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) data can be integrated at different stages of wind power forecasting. Proposed a stage-wise framework incorporating NWP through representation learning, latent-level fusion, and forecast correction, with an NWP bias-correction strategy. Conducted extensive experiments across multiple forecasting horizons, NWP error levels, and wind fluctuation scenarios. Preliminary results demonstrate that the choice of NWP integration stage significantly affects forecasting accuracy and model robustness.

Time Series Representation Learning Feature Fusion NWP
2025

NIPT-Based Fetal Anomaly Detection & Optimal Screening Timing

Programmer · CUMCM Mathematical Modeling Contest (高教社数学建模)

Built a four-stage pipeline: multiple regression to model relationships between DNA concentration, gestational age, and BMI; K-means clustering for BMI-based timing optimization; random survival forest for predicting success rates and optimizing detection windows with censored data; random forest classification for high-dimensional discrimination of trisomy in female fetuses.

Mathematical Modeling K-means Random Forest Survival Analysis
