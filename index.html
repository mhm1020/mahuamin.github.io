Research Experience

2026

NWP Integration Methods for Wind Power Forecasting

Project Lead · Undergraduate Innovation & Entrepreneurship Program (大创)

Investigated how Numerical Weather Prediction (NWP) data can be integrated to improve wind power forecasting accuracy. Systematically categorized existing strategies into three stages: NWP representation learning, latent feature fusion, and prediction correction. Designed controlled comparative experiments to validate each approach.

Wind Power Forecasting NWP Representation Learning Feature Fusion
2025

NIPT-Based Fetal Anomaly Detection Optimization

Programming Lead · CUMCM (高教社数学建模)

Developed a four-stage pipeline for optimizing detection timing and classifying fetal abnormalities using NIPT data: (1) regression modeling, (2) K-means clustering for group optimization, (3) random survival forest for optimal timing, and (4) random forest for anomaly classification.

Mathematical Modeling K-means Random Forest Survival Analysis
